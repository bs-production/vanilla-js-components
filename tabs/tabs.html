Crawl Space Insulation

Fiberglass insulation installed between the floor joists in a crawl space is notorious for becoming weighed down with humidity and moisture. As it does, it will sag downwards, eventually falling uselessly to the ground.

Structural Repair For Sagging Crawl Spaces

Crawl spaces can experience structural damage for many reasons, the main reasons being the following:

  1. Your crawl space has too few structural supports.
  2. The structural supports are sinking into the soil.
  3. The structural wood in the crawl space is rotting.

Structural damage in a crawl space can manifest itself in the home in many ways, including uneven floors, jamming doors, and cracks in the drywall.

Common solutions for crawl space structural repair are often ineffective -- and can even lead to further structural damage as they're installed.
